Bald Eagle

Eagles are some of the largest birds. They are at the top of the food chain, with some species feeding on big prey like monkeys and sloths.

  • Scientific Name:Accipitridae
  • Average Length:36-43 inches
  • Average Lifespan:20 to 30 years
  • Habitat:Wetland

Bald eagles are large, predatory raptors that are recognizable for their brown body and wings, white head and tail, and hooked yellow beak. Their feet, which are also yellow, are equipped with sharp black talons.
